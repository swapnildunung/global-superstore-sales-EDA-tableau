## Project Overview

This project presents an interactive Tableau dashboard built to analyze sales performance, profitability, customer behavior and regional market trends using the Global Superstore dataset. The dashboard enables stakeholders to monitor key business KPIs, identify growth opportunities and support strategic decision-making through data visualization.

---

## Business Problem

A global retail company wanted to understand sales performance across product categories, customer segments and geographic markets while identifying opportunities to improve profitability, optimize discount strategies and support business growth.

---

## Objectives

- Analyze multi-year sales trends across product categories.
- Evaluate profitability by category and sub-category.
- Understand customer segment contribution.
- Compare discount patterns across product categories.
- Analyze quantity purchasing behavior.
- Evaluate regional market preferences.
- Build an interactive executive dashboard for business decision-making.

## Key Business Insights

- Consumer segment contributes over **51%** of total sales, making it the primary revenue driver.
- Technology products, particularly **Phones** and **Copiers**, generate the highest sales contribution.
- Tables receive the highest average discount, highlighting opportunities to optimize pricing strategies.
- Sales demonstrate consistent year-over-year growth across all major product categories.
- Asia Pacific and Europe exhibit distinct product preferences, supporting region-specific marketing and inventory planning.
- Customer purchases are concentrated in lower quantity ranges, indicating frequent small-volume transactions.

---

## Business Recommendations

- Prioritize Consumer-focused marketing initiatives to maximize revenue growth.
- Optimize discount strategies for heavily discounted product categories.
- Increase inventory investment in high-performing Technology products.
- Implement region-specific marketing based on customer preferences.
- Leverage dashboard insights for continuous KPI monitoring and strategic planning.
